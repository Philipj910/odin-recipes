The Perfect Air Fried Potatoes Recipe

Potatoes, Nutritious and Delicious

Whether they are baked, fried, mashed, there are numererous potatoes can be prepared. One such way is with an air fryer. In this recipe you will learn how to create the perfect air fried potatoes that will satisfy your hunger. This recipe will show you the ingredients and instructions that you can satisfy your hunger!

Ingredients

  • Baby Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Air Fryer (Obviously)

Instructions

  1. Cut up your potatoes into bite sized pieces.
  2. Pour potatoes into a mixing bowl and add a few Tbsp of olive oil.
  3. Mix until potatoes are fully coated
  4. Place in air fryer and cook until golden brown
  5. Finished!
